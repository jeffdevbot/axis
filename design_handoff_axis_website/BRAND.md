# Axis Brands — Brand Rules

## Positioning
Operator-led Amazon & Walmart growth partner. Premium B2B. Operator-to-operator voice.
Reference aesthetic: **Linear meets Stripe** — quiet confidence, dense data, generous white space, no hype.

## Voice
- "We" and "you" — never "I", never "they" about Axis
- Em dash is the signature punctuation — use liberally
- Short sentences over clever ones
- Stats carry the brag; body copy stays level
- Negative KPIs use a true minus (−), not a hyphen (-)

## Color usage
- **Navy (`--ink-800`) is earned.** Use only for hero, nav, footer, final CTA band. Never as a generic dark section.
- **Azure (`--azure-600`) is the conversion color.** One primary CTA per section. Never a large surface.
- **Cream (`--surface-100`)** is the "section divider" — use to break up white expanses.
- **Slate** is the text hierarchy below ink.

## Don't
- No emoji
- No gradients (exception: the single cool-blue radial glow behind the hero H1 and final CTA)
- No warm tones (oranges, warm reds) — Axis is cool-palette only
- No stock photography with smiling people around laptops
- No icon-per-bullet-point — icons earn their place

## Do
- Use monospace (`JetBrains Mono`) for stat labels, meta copy, timestamps
- Use real numbers wherever possible
- Let whitespace carry weight — padding around sections is 96–128px vertical on desktop
- Photos should feel editorial — tight crops, neutral backgrounds, slight desaturation on team photos

## Primary CTA label
Everywhere: **"Book a 15-Minute Call"**. Don't shorten, don't rephrase.
